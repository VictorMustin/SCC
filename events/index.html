Group Rides

Registration Mandatory

Join our weekly group ride for fun and friendly afterwork rides!

We offer two different groups to accommodate all levels:

  • The first one is more sporty with an average of 32km/h, games and a small competition on the course. But don't worry, we're here to have fun above all! We always wait for each other at the top of the hills, it's an way to hype each other up.
  • The second group is more relaxed, with a constant pace to chat with friends and enjoy the scenery. So don't hesitate anymore, join us for some unforgettable bike outings.

But either way, everybody's welcome to share a beer at the end!

Looking to up your game and get some race experience?

We include several categories so that everyone can compete at their own level. Also, we host before every event a crit clinic with expert coaches to provide guidance and tips.

  • Low pressure race training for competitive riders
  • Great first taste of competition and high intensity for intermediate riders
  • For beginners, it's a chance to improve their bike handling skills and gain confidence on the road.

But most importantly, it's a fun and relaxed atmosphere to hang out with the Brussels cycling community and make progress together.

Brussels Crit is a community-focused event that encourages daily bike use and promotes sportsmanship.

Held in the prestigious Laeken Park, this gathering is a great show for everyone to watch, with riders from across Belgium and Europe competing across different categories, including beginners and professionals, on the same track and facing the same challenges.
